Roman Numerals Converter

This is an app that transforms Roman Numerals in Deciman Numbers and vice versa.

To use the app just enter the desired numeral or number in the coresponding input field from below and hit the "Convert" button.

For the Roman Numerals input the accepted characters you can use are as follows:

  • I = 1
  • V = 5
  • X = 10
  • L = 50
  • C = 100
  • D = 500
  • M = 1000
  • (V) = 5000
  • (X) = 10000
  • (L) = 50000
  • (C) = 100000
  • (D) = 500000
  • (M) = 1000000

The characters wrapped between parentheses , ie. "(X)", mean that the value of the roman numeral between the parentheses is multiplied by 1000.
ie. X = 10
therefore (X) = 10 * 1000 = 10000

For the Decimal Numbers input the accepted numbers are in the interval [1, 3999999]

*if the text in the fields is too long you can scroll horizontaly.
On PC use the arrows and on devices with touchscreen slide on the field.
